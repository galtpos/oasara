# Accept Payments (Provider) - Simple Tutorial Script

## Advisory Board Design Principles Applied

- **Joseph Coughlin**: Large text, clear steps for clinic staff
- **Don Norman**: One action per screen, clear affordances
- **Ruth Clark**: Segment into chunks, visual + narration
- **Jony Ive**: Minimal - only essential information
- **Paulo Freire**: Emphasize provider autonomy and sovereignty

---

## Video Script (6 Steps, ~60 seconds)

### Step 1: Open Your Wallet (5 sec)
**Screen**: Zano wallet dashboard
**Narration**: "Open your Zano wallet. This is how you'll receive payments from patients."
**Action**: Red circle highlights wallet balance area

---

### Step 2: Go to Receive (5 sec)
**Screen**: Wallet with Receive tab highlighted
**Narration**: "Tap Receive. This shows your payment address."
**Action**: Red circle on Receive button

---

### Step 3: Your Payment Address (10 sec)
**Screen**: Receive screen with QR code and address
**Narration**: "Here's your unique address. The QR code makes it easy - patients just scan and pay. Tap Copy to share the address directly."
**Action**: Circle on QR code and Copy button

---

### Step 4: Share with Patient (8 sec)
**Screen**: Share options or messaging app
**Narration**: "Send your address or QR code to the patient. They can pay instantly from anywhere in the world. No bank approval. No waiting."
**Action**: Arrow showing share action

---

### Step 5: Payment Arrives (10 sec)
**Screen**: Wallet showing incoming transaction notification
**Narration**: "When payment arrives, you'll see it immediately. The transaction confirms in about two minutes. No chargebacks. No freezes. The money is yours."
**Action**: Circle on incoming payment notification

---

### Step 6: Confirmed - You're Paid (8 sec)
**Screen**: Wallet showing confirmed balance
**Narration**: "That's it. You've just received a private payment. No bank fees. No payment processor taking a cut. No government reporting your transaction. This is financial freedom for your practice."
**Action**: Circle on updated balance

---

## Total Runtime: ~46 seconds

## Key Messages (per Paulo Freire - Liberation Pedagogy)

1. **Simple as email** - Share address, receive payment
2. **Instant settlement** - No waiting for bank processing
3. **Zero chargebacks** - Unlike credit cards
4. **No middlemen** - No processor fees
5. **Your money, your control** - True ownership
